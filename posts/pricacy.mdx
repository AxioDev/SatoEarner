1. Collection of Information: 
SatoEarner collects minimal user data, such as the user's device type, IP address, and usage statistics to enhance the user experience and to deliver personalized content.

2. Use of Information: 
The data collected by SatoEarner is solely used to improve the application's performance, for advertising purposes, and to deliver relevant content to users. We do not share, sell, or disclose user data to third-party entities for commercial purposes.

3. Security: 
We take the security of user data seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect it. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

4. Compliance: 
SatoEarner complies with all relevant laws and regulations governing the collection, use, and storage of user data, including GDPR and COPPA.

5. User Control: 
Users have the right to access, modify, and delete their data, and can do so by contacting our customer support team.
